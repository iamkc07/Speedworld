Sports bikes

A Sports bike is a type of motorcycle which falls under the performance-bike segment. The manufacturers optimize the sports bikes for acceleration (pick-up), speed, braking, and cornering abilities.
However, these performance parameters come at the cost of comfort and fuel economy compared to other motorcycles. So, generally, most of the Sports motorcycles worldwide have varying sizes and prices than other bikes.
Traditionally, Sports bikes have narrow and hard seats. Besides, the riding position allows feet to rest at the rear while hands bend in elbows on such bikes. Thus, it forces the rider to lean forward; as if virtually sleeping on the bike. Therefore, it helps to reduce the aerodynamic drag. So, the spine remains bent forwards while riding a sports bike.
Moreover, the cornering ability in these bikes is immense because of the low center of gravity and low ground clearance. Hence, manufacturers design sports bikes for short distances but at high speeds, such as during racing events.
